Let's Tech

Come on ladies, let's techover!

What is a techover?

A techover is when you take over the technology industry. In this male dominate field, there should be more women, so why not include ourselves in the data. The only way that we can do this is to techover, by studying and gaining experience, to get more jobs/careers in the tech field.

Why?

With more women in tech, there will be more diversity. Regardless of what job you hold, if it is in the tech field, you are woman in tech. This alone can help us be more represented while breaking down barriers and techingover!
